About Helpo Nigeria

Our mission to transform lives across Nigeria

Who We Are

Helpo Nigeria is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving lives and empowering communities across all 36 states of Nigeria. Founded in 2010, we've impacted over 500,000 lives through our various initiatives.

Our team consists of passionate Nigerians working alongside international partners to create sustainable solutions to the challenges facing our communities.

Our Services

How we're making a difference in Nigeria

Education Programs

Building schools, training teachers, and providing scholarships to underprivileged children across Nigeria.

Healthcare Initiatives

Mobile clinics, vaccination campaigns, and healthcare education in rural communities.

Clean Water Projects

Borehole drilling and water purification systems for communities without access to clean water.

Agricultural Support

Training and resources for sustainable farming practices to combat food insecurity.

Disaster Relief

Emergency response and rehabilitation for communities affected by floods, conflicts, and other disasters.

Women Empowerment

Vocational training and microloans to help Nigerian women achieve financial independence.
